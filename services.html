Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about our training programs and services.

What is the duration of each training session?

Our training sessions typically last for 60 minutes, but we also offer tailored session durations based on individual needs.

Are nutritional consultations included in the training programs?

Yes, our personalized training programs include comprehensive nutritional guidance and meal planning to complement your fitness goals.

Do you offer specialized programs for seniors?

Absolutely! We design fitness programs that cater to the unique needs and concerns of senior individuals, promoting strength, mobility, and independence.

Can I sign up for a trial session before committing to a training program?

Yes, we offer trial sessions to allow you to experience our training approach firsthand and determine if it aligns with your fitness objectives.

What safety measures are in place during group training sessions?

We prioritize the safety and well-being of our clients with heightened sanitation protocols and attentive supervision during group training sessions.
